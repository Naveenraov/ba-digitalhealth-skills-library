## About This Skill

### What This Skill Is About

Spectrum.Life is growing fast. 500+ employees today. 100 new roles in Australia over the next 12 months. Clinical leads joining from Valion, MindFit and We Lysn acquisitions. New BAs, engineers and clinicians across Ireland, UK and Australia — all starting in a company with a deeply specific culture, a complex AI product ecosystem and a care-first mission that needs to be felt from day one.

Every new hire's first experience of Spectrum.Life shapes whether they stay, engage and contribute — or feel lost and disconnect. A generic onboarding plan sends the wrong signal at the most important moment.

This skill generates a personalised, role-specific 30-day onboarding plan for any new Spectrum.Life hire — covering weekly milestones, product knowledge, culture touchpoints, key introductions, success criteria and a manager checklist.

### The Real Problem This Solves

Currently onboarding at Spectrum.Life is inconsistent. Different departments onboard differently. A new clinician in Belfast gets a different experience from a commercial hire in Dublin. A MindFit team member in Melbourne gets a different experience from a new hire in Dublin HQ. With 100 new Australian roles coming — onboarding at scale without a structured approach is a real retention and culture integration risk.

This skill gives every manager a high-quality, role-specific 30-day plan in minutes — consistently applied across all locations, all departments and all roles.

### When This Skill Comes Into Picture

A new hire has accepted their offer. The HR team or line manager wants to prepare a structured first 30 days. They trigger this skill with the new hire's role, department, location and context — and receive a complete onboarding plan covering Day 1 through Day 30 with daily milestones, key introductions, product knowledge focus areas, culture touchpoints and success criteria.

### What It Gives The Team

New hires feel valued and set up for success from day one. Managers get a high-quality plan in minutes. The HR team gets consistent onboarding standards across five locations. And Spectrum.Life's low ego high ambition culture lands properly — because it is deliberately built into every plan.

### The Before and After

**Before this skill** — onboarding varies by manager. New hires in different locations get inconsistent experiences. Culture is communicated informally. Product knowledge is ad-hoc. At scale with Australian expansion, this becomes a real retention risk.

**After this skill** — every new hire gets a structured, personalised, culture-rich 30-day plan. Consistent across Dublin, Belfast, UK and Australia. Role-specific and department-relevant. Built on Spectrum.Life's actual values, products and ways of working.

> Onboarding is the first chapter of an employee's Spectrum.Life story. This skill makes sure that chapter is written with the same care and quality that Spectrum.Life puts into every member interaction.
